Neuromuscular Therapy

The body's central nervous system is essentially the wiring that carries signals from the brain to the body. Neuromuscular therapy is a specialized deep tissue massage designed to relieve areas of muscle strain. These areas will typically feel tender and even trigger remote areas of discomfort. By applying pressure to these areas, neuromuscular massage therapy can help release muscle tension and soreness and reeducate the muscle to offer continual relief. Unlike other massage techniques, neuromuscular massage often consists of static, or near static, application of pressure.

Our experienced therapists at Natural Earth Healing Center always pay close attention to the client's needs during this and every massage technique we practice.

Myofascial Release Massage

By applying sustained pressure and deliberate movement, a myofascial release massage is directed toward the connective tissues around the muscles called fascia. This tissue interconnects each part of the body. When fascial tension is found, pressure is applied to release it. This common massage therapy is shown to be beneficial in relieving cervical, back, and other chronic pain. Myofascial release massage can also be an effective therapy for fibromyalgia, scoliosis, neurological dysfunction, and other restrictions of motion.
